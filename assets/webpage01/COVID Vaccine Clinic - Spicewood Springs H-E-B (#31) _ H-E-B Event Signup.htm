COVID Vaccine Clinic - Spicewood Springs H-E-B (#31)

COVID Vaccinations at H-E-B Pharmacy

We are currently accepting appointments for Phase 1a, Hospital and Community Healthcare Workers, First Responders, Last Responders, and Phase 1b, those age 65+ or age 18+ with a chronic condition.


If you do not fall into these priority groups, you should not schedule an appointment. Our vaccine supply is limited and we are trying to get to those with the greatest need. As we complete this category and have a better supply of vaccine, we will have more events. Please check vaccine.heb.com for the latest information on vaccine availability.


Please bring an ID that matches your appointment name, and wear a mask when coming for your appointment.


**This is for FIRST (1st) dose only; your second (2nd) dose appointment will be automatically scheduled after you receive your first dose**


*You are scheduling to receive the Moderna COVID 19 Vaccine*


NOTE: If you received your first dose somewhere other than H-E-B, please contact that provider about receiving your second (2nd) dose. We are only guaranteed to receive sufficient 2nd dose allocation to vaccinate customers that received their first dose with us.
